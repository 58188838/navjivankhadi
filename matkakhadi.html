Matka khadi

Measurements

- Length - 1mtr, Width - 45in

Texture/Material

- Coarse Texture Khadi Matka Silk Fabric Matka is made from waste silk. The first & last part of the silk does not have the same shine as the center and is usually disposed of, typically it’s burned or thrown away in a landfill. We yarn dye the “waste silk” into two different colors, twist the two colors into one yarn and then weave it into fabric, creating a tweed-like appearance. Khadi is a fabric made by hand-spinning threads on a ‘charkha’, these threads can be cotton, silk or wool. The fabric keeps you warm in the winter, cool in the summer and lasts for years. Easy to maintain and giving it a lustrous appearence.
